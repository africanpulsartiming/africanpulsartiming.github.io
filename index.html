Research and Projects

A key science goal of Pulsar Timing Array research groups, like the APT, is to study a network, or an array, of radio pulsars and search for deviations in their expected pulse rates. Such deviations could reveal the presence of low frequency gravitational waves disturbing our modeling of their clockwork.
Beyond PTA-directed science, APT members are also involved in a range of other pulsar-related science projects with applications from Gravity to Cosmology. Find out more here!
